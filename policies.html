Policies

COVID-19

In-person sessions

Due to the ongoing global pandemic, I do not currently offer in-person sessions. All sessions will be held virtually.

Subjects and Services

Subjects

  • Javascript game design/Introduction to game development

  • Study habits

What I provide

As your tutor, there are some things that are, and others that are not part of my job. For example, it is my job to:

  • Break down and explain concepts in a clear, understandable way
  • Encourage you and help you feel confident in your understanding
  • Effectively and completely answer your questions
  • Help you figure out material you've been struggling with
  • Treat you with respect, kindness, and dignity

It is not my job to:

  • Do your homework for you, although I'm happy to walk through similar problems with you
  • Promise you a certain grade, although I will give you the tools you need to get there
  • Keep track of your assignments for you, although I have many great tips for time-management and organization

I am human

Though I am very knowledgeable about math and computer science, there's always more to learn. There may be times when I don't know the answer to your question, or I make a mistake. I'm only human! My promise to you is that I will use these situations as an opportunity to model how to overcome academic challenges, and ensure we both come out with a better understanding.

Scheduling

Availability

I am available throughout the week and weekend, although my specific availability varies. Reach out to me and we can work together to find a time that works for you.

Recurring sessions

You can schedule either one-time meetings or regularly scheduled sessions - whatever floats your boat!

How we meet

I am flexible with how we meet. I've used many platforms, including Zoom, Skype, Discord, WebEx, and even just a phone call. We can use whatever you're most comfortable with, except FaceTime - I'm on Android!

Late policy

Life is full of surprises, I completely understand that. If you're running late, I'm happy to wait as long as you need until you arrive, but the meeting will still end as scheduled. I cannot add "extra time" at the end of a session to make up for missed time at the start.

Cancellation policy

If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, that's no problem. You may cancel or reschedule at no penalty up to 24 hours before the start of an appointment. After 24 hours, I will charge for the full amount of the session out of respect for both my time and other students who may have scheduled an appointment then.

Ethics

Academic integrity

As a tutor, it is my job to aid and strengthen your education - not to undermine it. To that extent, I will abide by both your school's and your class's academic integrity policies to the letter. I am entirely unwilling to help you cheat. Please look elsewhere if your goal is to find someone to just take your exams for you.

Respect

This one's pretty simple. I will treat you with the respect and kindness you deserve, and I ask that you do the same for me. Life is so much better when we're all nice to each other :)

Statement of non-discrimination

I'm happy to be your tutor, no matter your background, age, or level of experience. I do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, gender identity, age, national origin, disability, marital status, sexual orientation, or military status. We're all human, and we all deserve respect.

Payment

Time of payment

Payment is expected within 2 days of an appointment. Payment in advance is welcome but not required.

Method of payment

I accept PayPal, CashApp, Venmo, or cash (once in-person sessions resume). Please include a message that makes it clear the payment is for a tutoring session, and include the date of the session.

Privacy

Contact information

As a part of our tutor-student relationship, I will ask you for the best way to contact you. Any contact information you choose to provide, as well as your personal information, will be kept private and not shared with anyone.

Recording sessions

I am okay with you recording audio and/or video of our sessions for personal use, under the condition that all recordings be kept private and are not used outside of your own personal review and study. I also ask that you give me a quick heads-up that you will be recording.

Confidentiality

As part of our tutor-student relationship, I will treat everything we discuss during our sessions as confidential, and will not share anything except in the following very unusual circumstances:

  • I am required by law to provide confidential information
  • I feel that by sharing confidential information, I might prevent you or others from serious and imminent harm
  • I have requested and obtained explicit permission from you
